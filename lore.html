Game Lore

Project: Glorbo represents a story of mad scientists who took it too far.

The first part of their experiment was to pin members of the military against each other.

Then, they brought life to the military's machines.

Next, they turned to the use of magic, but that still didn't satisfy the scientists.

The angels had finally had enough of all this, and they came down in an attempt to put an end to it all.

At the same time, the demons rose up and were able to trick the humans into trying one more experiment.

And it went HORRIBLY wrong, creating a series of parasitic monsters known as Glorbo.
