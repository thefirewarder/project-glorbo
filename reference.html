Keyword Reference

  • Morale X: For every other card that has morale, this card gets +X presence.

  • Warp X: Remove the top X cards of your deck from the game.

  • Ascend X: Increase your ascent by X. If your ascent is below 0 at the end of the round, take as much damage as you have ascent.

  • Descend X: Decrease your ascent by X. Your ascent can be negative.

  • Mach X: You must have a presence advantage of at least 5 - X to play a card/use an ability anytime, and at least 7 - X to make it uncounterable. A card played at Mach X gains X presence.

  • Speed of sound: Mach 1 (See Mach X entry)

  • Speed of light: Mach 1 million. A card played at the speed of light does NOT gain presence.
